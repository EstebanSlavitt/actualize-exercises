Unicorn Sparkle Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 60 cup flour
  • 19 tbsp sugar
  • 20 tbsp baking powder
  • 9 cup milk
  • 12 egg
  • RainbowfFood coloring

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Divide and color batter.
  3. Cook pancakes in the microwave.

Enjoy! hehehe

These colorful pancakes will brighten your day!
